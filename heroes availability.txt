NOTE: 
1- In some maps, you can transform your regular 
units 
into hero units, by bringing them into a 
beacon near to your initial base.


2- The availability of heroes depend on the 
map, each map a different set of heroes.


3- You can only have a specific number of heroes 
of each type at the same time, the limit also 
depends on the map.


4- Check this information in the section 
below, you will find the heroes that you can 
have per mission, and the limit of each 
hero.
You can repeat this process all the times 
you want, that means if one of your heroes die 
you can replace it with another one meanwhile 
you don't excess the limit.
